Before you start!

Make sure you are hydrated, well-rested, and in a quiet spot before you take this diagnosis. You will be asked to read a few paragraphs of text out loud for a minute. This text is randomly generated. Be sure to read in your normal voice as if you are speaking to a pearson a couple of feet from you. Make sure your entire face, especially your eyes, is visible in the camera frame the entire time.

When you are ready to begin, press the button below.

When you begin diagnosis. The text will appear here. Simply keep reading until the text dissapears. Your video and audio will always be in the sidebar for your reference.

Make sure your camera can see your face, and that your microphone is picking up audio below.

   Predicting

Congratulations! You have finished the test.

Parkin-Sense is now predicting your chance of having Parkinson's Disease. This may take several minutes (up to 5). Do not close or reload this page. It will automatically update when the results are calculated.

If you would like the cancel your results, or restart the diagnosis, press the button below. This action cannot be undone!
